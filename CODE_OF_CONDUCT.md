# Code of Conduct

RawBridge uses a practical Contributor Covenant style policy.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about bugs and risks.
- Keep technical discussions focused on reproducible facts.
- Welcome photographers, designers, agencies, and developers with different levels of experience.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, publishing private information, or repeatedly derailing project work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and contributions that violate this policy. Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to `GitHub Issues or maintainer contact` until a permanent maintainer contact is published.
